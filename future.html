Our Future Plans

More plants and trees
  • We would love to expand the variety of plants and trees accessible in the game for engagement, as mental health is a crucial issue and a broader range of visuals can create more soothing, personalized experiences.
  • More exploration, encouraging players to stay curious and discover new plant types as rewards for healthy habits
  • Each plant can resonate with the players, allowing different emotions
Pets and animals
  • Numerous individuals in life find themselves lonely in life. This new expansion in the game would allow them to fill that lonely gap with a pet.
  • Similar to real life, pets often help improve the mental state and lives of many individuals.
  • Creates a similarity between themselves and others who also have pets.
Multiplayer Feature
  • Allow players to work together towards improving their mental health.
  • Creating a healthy companionship/friendship within the game.
